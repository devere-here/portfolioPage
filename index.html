About Me

I'm a recent college grad with a background in Physics and Math. For the longest time I wanted to become a teacher, but as I became more exposed to computer science in college I became more enamored with software. When I graduated college I knew that I wanted to become a computer programmer. As a result, I spent months teaching myself web development and then attended the Fullstack Academy coding bootcamp where I learn more advanced programming concepts and got to work on large projects with other developers. I'm now working as a freelancer in New York.
